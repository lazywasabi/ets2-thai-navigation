Thai Voice Navigation 5.1.0

REQUIRED ETS2 OR ATS VERSION 1.43 - 1.49

- 3 Voices:
  - Google TTS (Same as Google Maps)
  - Microsoft TTS
  - Yuki (Audio by Yuki & hiyamikan)
- Support Euro Truck Simulator 2 and American Truck Simulator
- Support FMOD sound engine
- Support all commands including sound and voice for speed limit warning

Website: github.com/lazywasabi/ets2-thai-navigation
